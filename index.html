Personal Profile

I'm a bit of a hybrid with a passion for Web-based technologies combined with a talent for improving business processes and UX.

Passionate for all things Web-based. The more exposure I've had to the cloud and web-based IT, the more my interest in web technologies has grown. Merging my passions of business and technology has led me to be involved in many various positions including Project Management, Business Analysis, and most recently Front End Web Development.
